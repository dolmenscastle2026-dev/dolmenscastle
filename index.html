Frequently Asked Questions — Dolmens Castle Homestay

Where is Dolmens Castle Homestay located?

Dolmens Castle Homestay is located in Marayoor, on Kanthaloor Road, Idukki District, Kerala — PIN 685620. We are approximately 35–40 km from Munnar town and 10 km from Kanthaloor village, nestled in the hills of the Western Ghats.

What are the room rates at Dolmens Castle?

Rooms start from ₹1,499/night for the Deluxe Room (couples). The Executive Suite for 12–20 guests costs ₹14,999–₹19,999/night. The Presidential Suite for 20–25 guests is ₹25,000/night. Rates may vary by season — contact us on WhatsApp for the latest offers.

What tourist places can we visit from Dolmens Castle?

From our homestay you can easily visit Thoovanam Waterfalls, Marayoor Sandalwood Forest, Kanthaloor Apple Orchards, Munnar Tea Estates, Eravikulam National Park, Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary, Mattupetty Dam, Neelakurinji viewing area, and Pampadum Shola National Park.

Is Dolmens Castle suitable for large groups and family trips?

Absolutely! Our Executive Suite (12–20 guests) and Presidential Suite (20–25 guests) are ideal for large family holidays, group tours, and corporate retreats. We also arrange group activities like campfire, guided treks, and Kerala-style feasts.

What is check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability. Please contact us in advance.
